Visit West London

Enjoy culture, history, and shopping in the 'fancy' side of London!

West and Best rhyme for a reason

Come to Carnival!

Every year, the area is host to the vibrant Notting Hill Carnival, a celebration to the local Caribbean community! All are welcome to see the parade and take part in a variety of activities.

See lovely Little Venice!

Set sail down London's own Venetian canals... or take in the beauty with a nice walk along the towpaths! There is a yearly festival here in May to celebrate the heritage of London canals!

Get lost in a Royal Park!

West London is home to a number of parks once owned by the King. Check them out for horse riding, wild swimming, debate economics where Karl Marx once stood - or have a picnic!
